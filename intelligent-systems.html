No. During installation, we train the system on your voice, your frameworks, your stories. We pull from your calls, your posts, your way of explaining things. The output sounds like you on your best day. Clients regularly can't tell what was AI-assisted and what wasn't.
"Will this replace my team?"
Your team doesn't go away. They get superpowers. A 3-person marketing team doing 10 pieces a week can do 60+ with the right AI infrastructure underneath them. We don't replace your people. We multiply their output so you can stop hiring and start scaling what you already have.

You own everything. The systems, the agents, the workflows, the dashboards. It all lives inside your business. Most clients stay on a retainer because we keep optimizing and adding new capabilities. But you're never locked in. Walk away and everything still runs.

ChatGPT is a tool. We install infrastructure. That's like buying a hammer and saying you tried building a house. "What industries does this work for?"
We've built systems for agencies, consultants, SaaS, e-commerce, professional services, construction, and trades businesses. The system adapts because we build it around your specific workflows, not a template. That said, we screen every applicant. If we don't think we can deliver results, we'll tell you on the call.
