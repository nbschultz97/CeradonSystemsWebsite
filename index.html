Pose Intelligence Pipeline

CSI-to-skeleton in four disciplined stages

  1. 1. Passive radios capture raw CSI streams alongside inertial references without emitting a single watt of additional RF.
  2. 2. Edge DSP modules denoise, calibrate, and align subcarriers in real time, prepping tensors for pose estimators on Pi or Jetson hardware.
  3. 3. Quantized pose networks infer skeletal joints, body orientation, and stability states with deterministic latency budgets.
  4. 4. Structured outputs feed mission overlays, haptic alerts, and scripted CLI tasks without ever calling back to cloud services.

Each stage is modular, so supporting features—RSSI logging, mobility cues, device fingerprinting—slot in without disrupting the pose estimator.
