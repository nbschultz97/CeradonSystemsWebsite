Our Mission

Deliver decisive, low-signature sensing to teams operating at the edge.

Ceradon Systems, LLC is a Utah-based, founder-led engineering firm focused on Vantage, a passive Wi-Fi CSI sensing platform, and Aegis, a readiness assurance system for unmanned assets. Architect Stack is maintained as an open-source project by Noah Schultz (individual), built to plan COTS unmanned systems with offline-first workflows.

Founder

Noah Schultz — Founder (Utah). Noah is a career SOF veteran with experience across intelligence, robotics, and large Department of Defense acquisition programs. He focuses on offline-first RF sensing and planning tools that can run on low-power edge hardware.
