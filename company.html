Our Mission

Deliver decisive, low-signature sensing to teams operating at the edge.

Ceradon Systems is a security-focused engineering team building Vantage—passive Wi-Fi CSI sensing that renders skeletal pose intelligence in denied spaces. Every decision keeps operators safe, data local, and missions agile.

Founder

We prioritize modularity, CLI automation, and on-prem deployment so teams can integrate Vantage without compromising mission discipline.
