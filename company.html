Founder

Noah Schultz — Founder (Utah). Noah is a career SOF veteran with a special operations robotics background spanning intelligence, robotics, and large Department of Defense acquisition programs. He focuses on offline-first RF sensing and planning tools that can run on low-power edge hardware.
