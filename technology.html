Channel State Information (CSI)

Plain-language CSI: reading human movement from Wi-Fi

Every Wi-Fi packet carries rich multipath data. Vantage captures WiFi channel variations, applies signal conditioning and machine learning to classify human presence and movement — with skeletal pose estimation on the development roadmap.

Passive capture

We only listen. Commodity radios observe the environment’s response to existing Wi-Fi, keeping teams dark.

Detection-first pipeline

All feature extraction—from motion vectors to behavior scoring—supports presence detection today, with skeletal pose estimation planned for future releases.

Why passive matters

  • ▹ No new emissions means no EM signature to detect or jam.
  • ▹ Operates under EMCON policies and inside RF-denied structures.
  • ▹ Works where cameras fail—smoke, darkness, or obscured LoS.

Security & Ethics

Vantage never records video or audio. All processing stays local, logs are redacted for PII, and exports are encrypted. Passive sensing respects privacy while giving teams the pose intelligence needed to reduce collateral risk.

  • ▹ Signed firmware, secure boot, and tamper logging for field hardware.
  • ▹ Role-based access for dashboards and CLI tooling.
  • ▹ Export-control-aware configurations for compliant sharing.
